Crystal Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

when you click on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

Match your total score to the random number and you win. You lost the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is unknown until you click on them..
